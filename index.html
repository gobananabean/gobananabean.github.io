Stained Glass

Welcome to my stained glass informational website! Stained glass is an art form that I enjoy doing. I began doing it about two years ago. The process of creating a stained glass art piece involves an array of steps. The first step is picking out a pattern that you would like to follow. The second step is choosing which glass to use for the pattern. After this, comes foiling + soldering, or the step in which the glass is fused together, like an adhesive. Once your piece is soldered, some chemicals are applied to darken and strengthen it. Stained glass has brought me a lot of happiness as a creative outlet. It has given me the opportunity to connect with people and I look forward to improving and getting better over time! Check out the other pages on this website if you are interested in getting more information about how to make stained glass.
